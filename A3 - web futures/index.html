Togo Chair

The Togo Chair is one of the most recognisable designs from the early 1980's. Everybody just loves this chair design, with is low and soft exterior just inviting you to sit and relax with a good book or glass of wine.

The interior is crafted from High-density foam and the fabric has a stretch to it. The buttons and many folds on this lounge chair add to its charm and creates the retro look on this lounge chair.
